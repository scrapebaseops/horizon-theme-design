## Accessibility

- All images must have descriptive alt text (e.g., "Lettii cordless lamp glowing on a dinner table set for four")
- Text over images must meet WCAG AA contrast (4.5:1 minimum)
- Hero overlay opacity must be tested for readability
- Heading hierarchy: H1 (hero) → H2 (section headings) → H3 (column headings in Section 6)
- Focus states on CTA button
- Newsletter form: labelled input, visible focus, error states

## Decisions & Assumptions

1. **Problem-led, not founder-led**: The page never names a founder or tells a personal "journey" story. The narrative is about a frustration shared with the customer.
2. **300 words max body copy**: Draft copy above is approximately 280 words across all text sections. Lifestyle images do the rest.
3. **No "About Us" cliches**: No "passionate team," "our journey," "we believe in quality," or "founded in."
4. **Guarantee close pattern**: Consistent with the "guarantee sandwich" CRO pattern from research — guarantee appears at the end of every key page.
5. **Three lifestyle image breaks**: Alternating text/image pattern creates a magazine-feel scroll experience.
6. **No team photos or founder portraits**: Not appropriate for a brand selling atmosphere, not personality. Can be added later if the brand develops a personal following.
