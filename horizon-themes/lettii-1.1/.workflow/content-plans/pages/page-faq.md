### Section 2: FAQ Accordion — Product
**Shopify Section Type**: `collapsible-content` (accordion)
**Purpose**: Address all product-related objections. This is the primary objection-handling surface.

**Draft Content**:

**Category Heading**: "About the Lamps"

**Q: How long does the battery last?**
A: Up to 12 hours on low brightness, around 6 hours on full. That's a full dinner party, an evening in the garden, or a few nights on your bedside table — all from one charge. Recharging takes 4–5 hours via USB-C.

**Q: Is it bright enough to actually make a difference?**
A: Yes. Lettii lamps produce warm, diffused light — the same quality you see in restaurants and boutique hotels. They won't light a room on their own (that's not what they're for), but they transform the atmosphere of any table, shelf, or corner. Stepless dimming means you set exactly the level you want.

**Q: Can I use it outside? What if it rains?**
A: Every Lettii lamp is rated IP54 — that's protection against splashing water from any direction. Use it on the patio, the garden table, the balcony. A passing rain shower won't cause any problems. We wouldn't recommend submerging it, but normal outdoor use is exactly what it's designed for.

**Q: Will it look cheap or plasticky?**
A: This is the question we obsessed over. Lettii lamps use premium materials with a weighted base and quality finish. They're designed to look like they belong in a boutique hotel, not a novelty shop. The photos on our site are accurate — what you see is what arrives.

**Q: How does the dimming work?**
A: Touch the top of the lamp to turn it on and off. Touch and hold to dim smoothly — up and down. No app. No remote. No buttons. Just touch.

**Q: How do I charge it?**
A: USB-C cable, same as most modern phones. Plug it in, leave it for 4–5 hours, and you're set for the evening. A small LED indicator shows charging status. Cable included with every lamp.

**Q: Why should I pay more than an Amazon lamp?**
A: Fair question. Amazon has cordless lamps for £15–25. They also have thin plastic bodies, cool-toned LEDs, short battery life, and no design coherence. Lettii sits between those and £100+ designer brands — premium materials, warm light, real design, and a brand that actually answers your emails. The 30-day guarantee means you can compare side by side, risk-free.

### Section 5: FAQ Accordion — Care & Maintenance
**Shopify Section Type**: `collapsible-content` (accordion)
**Purpose**: Help customers get the most from their lamp and reduce avoidable returns.

**Draft Content**:

**Category Heading**: "Care & Maintenance"

**Q: How do I clean my lamp?**
A: Wipe with a soft, damp cloth. Avoid abrasive cleaners or solvents. The IP54 rating means it handles water splashes, but don't soak or submerge it.

**Q: Can I leave it charging overnight?**
A: Yes. The lamp has built-in overcharge protection — it stops drawing power once fully charged. No need to babysit it.

**Q: How long will the battery last before it needs replacing?**
A: The rechargeable battery is rated for 500+ full charge cycles. With typical use (charging 2–3 times per week), that's several years of life before any noticeable capacity reduction.

**Q: Can I replace the bulb?**
A: Lettii lamps use integrated LEDs — there's no bulb to replace. LED life is rated at 50,000+ hours. At 4 hours per day of use, that's over 30 years.

Each `mainEntity` item should contain `Question` with `name` and `acceptedAnswer` with `text`. The schema should be generated dynamically from the accordion content, or hardcoded in the template if accordion content is static.

## Decisions & Assumptions

1. **All 6 objections addressed directly**: The FAQ answers every objection from the brand brief — plasticky look, battery life, brand trust, brightness, rain/outdoor use, and price vs Amazon.
2. **Category grouping**: Four categories (Product, Ordering, Returns, Care) matches customer mental models and reduces scanning time.
3. **Answers subtly reinforce trust**: Each answer doesn't just inform — it builds confidence. Battery answers mention specific hours. Waterproofing cites the IP54 rating. Returns emphasize "no questions asked."
4. **FAQ Schema is a priority**: For a new brand with limited organic visibility, FAQ rich results in Google search are a high-value, low-effort SEO win.
5. **Contact CTA at bottom**: Every FAQ page loses some visitors whose question isn't covered. The contact CTA catches them.
6. **No images on this page**: FAQ is a utility page. Speed and scannability matter more than lifestyle imagery here.
7. **Price vs Amazon answer is direct**: Doesn't dodge the question. Names the price gap, acknowledges it, and justifies it with specifics + guarantee.
