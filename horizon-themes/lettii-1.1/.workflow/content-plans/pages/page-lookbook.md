## Decisions & Assumptions

1. **Editorial magazine feel**: This is not a product grid. It's a content page. Each feature section reads like a magazine article with shoppable elements.
2. **4 permanent features + 1 seasonal slot**: The core features (Dinner Party, Garden, Bedroom, Drinks) are evergreen. The seasonal slot rotates with campaigns and seasons.
3. **100–150 words per feature**: Enough to paint a scene and sell the feeling, not enough to lose attention.
4. **Shoppable but not pushy**: Product links and collection CTAs appear at the end of each feature, not inline interrupting the narrative.
5. **Seasonal rotation capability**: The merchant must be able to update the seasonal slot without developer intervention. Configurable sections in Shopify's theme editor handle this.
6. **Instagram-worthy imagery is essential**: This page lives or dies on image quality. AI-generated lifestyle images must be high quality, diverse in setting, and feel natural — not synthetic.
7. **Pinterest-optimised**: Long-form content pages with beautiful images perform well on Pinterest. Consider adding Pinterest "Save" buttons to images.
8. **No video at launch**: Can be added to feature sections later for richer content.
