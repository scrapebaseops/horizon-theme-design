# Handoff Brief for Page Spec Sub-Agents

## Brand Snapshot
- **Name**: Lettii
- **What we sell**: Premium cordless rechargeable LED table lamps. Touch control, stepless dimming, USB-C charging, IP54 waterproof. ~20 designs across style families (Mushroom, Slim, Lantern, Sculptural, Geometric, Crystal).
- **Price range**: £39–69 individual lamps, £89–149 bundles/sets of 2–4
- **Primary conversion goal**: Buy a lamp or bundle directly from ad click-through
- **Path to purchase**: Ad → Product Page → Buy (60%) | Ad → Collection → Product → Buy (25%) | Google Search → Product → Buy (15%)

## Brand Voice
- **Personality**: Warm, confident, design-literate, direct, understated
- **Example sentences**:
  - "One lamp. Every setting. Restaurant to bedside to garden party."
  - "Touch to dim. USB-C to charge. That's it."
  - "We made the lamp you've seen in every boutique hotel — and made it yours."
  - "No wires. No wax. No reason not to."
- **Copy conventions**: Short sentences. No filler. No "cosy vibes" clichés. Sell the feeling, not the specs. Confident but never pushy. Design-literate but accessible (think Zara Home shopper, not interior designer).

## Target Customer
- **Primary**: Style-conscious women 28–45 in the UK. Active on Instagram/Pinterest, care about how their home looks and feels. Mid-to-upper household income. Will spend £40–70 on something that elevates their space.
- **Main problem**: They want warm, inviting, stylish lighting — especially when hosting — but real candles are messy/dangerous, wired lamps are cluttered, and cheap LED lights look terrible.
- **Key objections**:
  - "Will it look premium or cheap and plasticky?"
  - "How long does the battery actually last?"
  - "I've never heard of this brand — can I trust the quality?"
  - "Is it bright enough to make a difference?"
  - "Can I use it outdoors in rain?"
  - "Why pay more than Amazon prices?"

## Key Messages (Priority Order)
1. "Restaurant lighting, at home" — the core transformation promise
2. "One touch. Infinite settings." — simplicity and flexibility
3. "No cords. No wax. No compromise." — convenience without aesthetic sacrifice
4. "Charges like your phone" — USB-C, 10–12 hours, modern and familiar
5. "30-day guarantee. Love it or send it back." — risk reversal

## Trust Signals to Use
- 30-day money-back guarantee, free returns, no questions asked
- Free UK shipping on orders over £50
- IP54 waterproof rating (specific, verifiable)
- Battery life: up to 12 hours per charge
- USB-C charging (signals modernity)
- UK-based customer support (email within 12hrs, live chat 9–6 Mon–Fri)
- Lifestyle imagery in restaurant/hotel/wedding settings (implied professional endorsement)
- Secure checkout + payment method badges (Visa, Mastercard, PayPal, Apple Pay, Klarna)

## CRO Priorities
1. Cold traffic conversion — product page must convert first-visit ad clickers
2. Trust establishment — no reviews at launch; guarantee, specs, and lifestyle imagery carry the load
3. AOV uplift via bundles — move customers from £39–69 singles to £89–149 sets
4. Lifestyle imagery as primary persuasion — sell atmosphere, not lumens
5. Objection handling on product page — address all 6 objections where the purchase happens

## Structural Decisions (from Research)
- Product page IS the landing page for ad traffic — must introduce brand AND sell simultaneously
- "Shop by Setting" is the primary collection taxonomy (Dining, Garden, Bedroom, Restaurants)
- Bundles are first-class products with own PDPs — not variants on single-lamp pages
- Cart drawer (not cart page) is the primary cart surface — maintains purchase momentum
- Trust signals appear in 3+ locations per page: near ATC, mid-page, footer
- No empty review widgets at launch — hide until 5+ reviews exist
- B2B is a "quiet door" — footer links to Hospitality and Weddings pages, no homepage B2B
- Sticky ATC bar on mobile — 85%+ of Meta ad traffic is mobile
- Free shipping threshold at £50 — between single-lamp price and bundle price to incentivize bundles

## Trait-Specific Requirements
- **Bundle trait** → Homepage "Shop Sets" section, bundle upsell in cart drawer, "What's Included" block on bundle PDPs, "SET"/"Save X%" badges on collection cards
- **B2B trait** → Dedicated Hospitality and Weddings pages with enquiry forms, "Ordering for a venue?" link on product pages, Restaurants & Bars collection with B2B banner
- **Cold traffic trait** → Sticky mobile ATC, ad-matching hero images, above-fold price/trust, fast page load
- **Trust trait** → Announcement bar trust rotation, guarantee sandwich pattern, FAQ accordion for objections, no empty review widgets
- **Lifestyle trait** → Lifestyle-first image galleries, setting-based collections, Styling Ideas lookbook page, "Complete the Setting" cross-sell

## Policies (for Cart/Product/Footer Copy)
- **Shipping**: Free standard UK shipping on orders over £50 (2–4 business days). Under £50: £3.95. Express next-day: £5.95. UK only at launch.
- **Returns**: 30-day money-back guarantee. Free return shipping, no questions asked. Full refund within 5 business days. Exchanges available.
- **Support**: Email (reply within 12 hours). Live chat 9am–6pm Mon–Fri. Instagram DMs monitored daily. FAQ page.

## Secondary Conversion Goals
1. Upgrade to bundle / add second lamp (AOV uplift)
2. Email newsletter signup (retargeting non-converters, 10% off incentive)
3. Browse the collection (for visitors not ready to buy from a single ad)
4. Follow on Instagram (brand building, retargeting pool)
